Designed a throwback livery dedicated to Sun Devil Motorsports 1993 competition car, celebrating 40 years of the clubs existance for our 2nd Annual Southwest Shootout event. The project used Adobe Illustrator to design and map out the decals to scale so I could cut more efficiently.

Adobe Illustrator Vinyl Sun Devil Motorsports

This livery was months in the making. Starting immediately after our arrival after competition, I wanted to make a livery that would stand out from the rest of the FSAE world, and after a final push to get the car ready for our unveiling event, my biggest project ever was finally complete. This project was sponsored by RVINYL who gave our club the neccesary tools inorder to complete this project with the highest precision. Adobe Illustrator was used to map and plan all the decal and get intial measurement cuts.
